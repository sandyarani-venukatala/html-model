BIOLOGY LAB

Biology Lab


On entering, there is a colourful and panoramic view of various charts and multiple models of human anatomy. There is a plethora of model specimens used as teaching aids. We have a series of advanced microscopes for excellent visualisation and better-quality images which are powered by electric field. The lab is also very spacious and well designed with designated space for museum, faculty and library.

PHYSICS LAB

Physics Lab

School has a very spacious well-designed Physics Laboratory which provides facilities to the students to conduct various activities, experiments and projects. The well-equipped lab helps the students to develop scientific reasoning and understand scientific concepts

CHEMISTRY LAB

Chemistry Lab

The school has legacy of providing hands-on practical experience to the students. To fulfil this objective, Chemistry lab facilitates students to develop problem solving and critical thinking skills through exposure to experiments and lab apparatus. The lab is well-equipped with the latest and state of art infrastructure and apparatus which provides exposure to the students to practical aspects of Chemistry. Its size is adequate enough to accommodate a complete section at a time, with all safety measures in effect and under close supervision of competent staff.
